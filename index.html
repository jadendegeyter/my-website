about

Hi, I'm Jay—designer, photographer, and perpetual playlist-maker. Music has always been the thread that ties my creative life together. The same way I build a visual story through my photography or design, I build a soundtrack for my days. Every song I love feels like a snapshot: a memory, a mood, or a little portal into who I am at that moment. I don't listen to music passively—it's always intentional. Whether I'm on a long walk with my camera, late-night editing a project, or just hanging out with friends, the songs I choose become part of the atmosphere. Some of my playlists are about yearning, some are about letting go, and some are just for the joy of feeling alive. This blog is where I get to talk about my favorite songs—the ones that stick to me, inspire me, or make me feel seen. They're not just tracks on repeat; they're part of the bigger story of how I experience love, memory, growth, and everything in between.
